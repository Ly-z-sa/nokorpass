Frequently asked questions

How do I book a movie ticket?

Go to Movies, choose a film, pick your cinema, date, and showtime, then select your seats on the booking page. Sign in or create an account to complete payment and receive your ticket.

Where can I find my tickets after booking?

Open My Tickets while signed in. Each booking shows your movie, showtime, seats, and a QR code to present at the cinema.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept ABA Pay, ACLEDA Pay, Visa, Mastercard, and KHQR. Available options appear on the payment screen when you checkout.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

All ticket sales are final unless stated otherwise at checkout. For exceptional cases (duplicate charge, wrong showtime selected by mistake), email us with your booking details and we will review your request.

I did not receive a confirmation email

Check your spam folder first. Your ticket is always available under My Tickets when you are logged in with the same account used to book. If you still cannot find it, contact support with your name and booking time.

How do I use my ticket at the cinema?

Show the QR code from My Tickets at the entrance or concession desk. Arrive at least 15 minutes before the showtime so staff can scan your code and seat you comfortably.

How do I reset my password or update my profile?

Visit Account while signed in to update your display name and preferences. For password resets, use the forgot-password link on the sign-in page.

Is my personal data secure?

Yes. We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore with industry-standard security. Read our Privacy Policy for full details on how we handle your data.
